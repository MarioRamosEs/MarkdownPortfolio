Unity project for [Space Taco Studio](https://spacetaco.studio), in collaboration with [Oscar Villalobos](https://ovillalobos.es/)

Play to get the highest score. Beat your record by dodging obstacles and enemies that appear on screen and use both power-ups and abilities to overcome any obstacle.
Will you achieve the highest score?

[Download at Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.marioramos.neoflappypong&hl=es_419)
